10 facts about

Cats !

number one

Cats have excellent night vision and can see in very low light.

number two

Cats can sleep for about 12-16 hours a day.

number three

Cats use their tails to help with balance.

number four

Cats sweat only through their paw pads.

number five

Cats purr to show comfort or help reduce pain.

number six

Cats have flexable spine that helps them move quickly and smoothly.

number seven

Cats can make more than 100 different sounds.

number eight

Cats can rotate their ears to hear sounds from different directions.

number nine

Each cat has a unique nose pattern, like human fingerprint.

number ten

Cats can jump up to six times their body length.
